# Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

> ⚠️ **TEMPLATE — not legal advice.** This is a starting point for a processor DPA. Have counsel
> review before offering it to customers. It forms part of the Terms of Service when you process
> personal data contained in Cassettes.

Between **[Customer]** ("Controller") and **[LEGAL ENTITY]** ("Processor", "Cassette").

## 1. Roles
For personal data contained in Cassettes, the Customer is the **controller** and Cassette is the
**processor**. Cassette processes such data only on the Customer's documented instructions (these
terms and use of the Service).

## 2. Subject matter & duration
Processing of Cassette content to provide the record/replay registry and behaviour-drift checks, for
the duration of the subscription plus the post-termination export window.

## 3. Nature & purpose
Storage, retrieval, comparison, and drift analysis of recorded LLM/agent request and response data
to operate the Service.

## 4. Data categories & subjects
Whatever the Customer's application sends to / receives from LLM providers. The Customer is
responsible for what enters Cassettes and should avoid recording unnecessary personal or
special-category data. Data subjects may include the Customer's end users.

## 5. Processor obligations
Cassette will: (a) process only on documented instructions; (b) ensure personnel are bound by
confidentiality; (c) implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures; (d) assist
the Controller with data-subject requests and with security/DPIA obligations; (e) not engage
sub-processors except as listed, with notice of changes and a right to object.

## 6. Sub-processors
Cloudflare (hosting/storage), Stripe (billing identifiers only), GitHub (PR integration), [email
provider]. Current list at [URL]; [14] days' notice of additions.

## 7. International transfers
Conducted under appropriate safeguards (UK IDTA / EU SCCs) where applicable.

## 8. Security
Encryption in transit, access controls, secret management, signed webhooks, append-only audit log,
and breach response procedures.

## 9. Personal data breach
Cassette will notify the Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of a personal data
breach affecting Cassette content, with available details to support the Controller's obligations.

## 10. Data subject requests
Cassette will, taking into account the nature of processing, assist the Controller in responding to
data-subject requests, including deletion and export of relevant Cassettes.

## 11. Deletion / return
On termination, Cassette will delete or return Cassette content within [30] days, save where law
requires retention.

## 12. Audit
Cassette will make available information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allow reasonable
audits, subject to confidentiality and notice.

Signed for and on behalf of the parties on [DATE].
